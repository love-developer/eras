**"Chronicler"** can be unlocked via:
- C002: Create capsules for 7 consecutive days
- C003: Create at least 1 capsule per month for 6 months

**E001 (Midnight Chronicler)** is a hidden achievement. Users discover it by creating a capsule at exactly 3:00 AM.

Users can choose "No Title" in Settings to hide their equipped title if they prefer.
