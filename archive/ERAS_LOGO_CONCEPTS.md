## üìê CONCEPT 1: "The Hourglass Portal"
**Theme:** Time flowing through a dimensional gateway

### Visual Description:
- **Static Logo:** A geometric hourglass shape where the narrow center acts as a portal/gateway
- Top half: Solid form (the past - crystallized, defined)
- Bottom half: Scattered particles (the future - forming, emerging)
- The center "neck" is a glowing horizontal line that suggests a threshold
- Modern, minimalist linework with a single accent color (deep indigo or warm amber)

### Eras Gate Animation:
1. **Initial State (Closed):** Hourglass appears complete, center line is thin
2. **Opening:** The center line expands vertically, splitting the hourglass in two halves
3. **Open State:** Top and bottom halves slide apart vertically, revealing the content in the center gap
4. **Visual Effect:** Particles from top half "flow" down into bottom half during opening
5. **Timing:** 0.8s ease-out

### Why It Works:
- Instantly communicates "time" without being literal
- Portal metaphor fits the capsule delivery moment perfectly
- Particle flow creates mesmerizing animation
- Sophisticated, not playful - premium feel

### Color Palette:
- **Primary:** Deep Indigo (#1E1B4B) or Midnight Blue (#0F172A)
- **Accent:** Warm Amber (#F59E0B) or Soft Gold (#FCD34D)
- **Particles:** Gradient from accent to primary (web only, solid on mobile)

### Why It Works:
- Honors current brand equity but ELEVATES dramatically
- Eclipse = rare, special alignment (like the moment you receive a capsule)
- Orbital mechanics = natural passage of time
- Celestial = timeless, eternal, grand scale
- Portal at center = gateway to memories

### Color Palette:
- **Left Body (Past):** Warm Bronze (#D97706) with subtle texture
- **Right Body (Future):** Cool Silver (#E2E8F0) with smooth gradient
- **Center Portal:** Deep Space Blue (#0C4A6E) to Black, with white rim light

## üé¨ Animation Specifications (All Concepts)

### Technical Parameters:
```javascript
// Easing functions
const EASE_SMOOTH = 'cubic-bezier(0.4, 0.0, 0.2, 1)';
const EASE_SPRING = 'cubic-bezier(0.34, 1.56, 0.64, 1)';
const EASE_ANTICIPATION = 'cubic-bezier(0.36, 0, 0.66, -0.56)';

// Timings
GATE_OPEN_DURATION = 800-1500ms (varies by concept)
GATE_CLOSE_DURATION = 600ms (always faster closing)
BREATHING_PULSE = 2000ms infinite

// Mobile Considerations
- Use solid colors only (NO gradients)
- Reduce animation duration by 20% on mobile
- Simplify particle effects for performance
- Ensure 60fps on all devices
```

### Best Practices:
- **Anticipation:** Slight scale-down before opening (makes expansion more dramatic)
- **Follow-through:** Elements continue moving slightly after main motion stops
- **Layering:** Different elements move at different speeds for depth
- **Sound Design:** Each concept would benefit from subtle UI sound
- **Accessibility:** Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` setting
